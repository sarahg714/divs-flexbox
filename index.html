Hi, I'm Sarah.

I'm a Responsive Designer & Developer. My mantra is to "Keep Going, Keep Growing." I'm fairly new to designing but upon starting, I've found a passion for which I'm striving to constantly learn and do better.

Prior to web design, I worked in healthcare for years. While it was a very stimulating job, I found myself yearning for more creativity and flexibility. I found coding while meeting with a career counselor. It's been a perfect match since then.

I would describe my style of design as modern and minimal while trying to incorporate a touch of nature and whimsy. Think "flower blooming out of concrete" - let's keep things positive and optimistic!

When not coding, you can find me knitting, drinking tea while reading a good book, or fangirling BTS. I'm also mama to two sweet boys. There's always something to do, and I'm learning to take in and savor the moments as they come.
